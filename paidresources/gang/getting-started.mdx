## Getting Started

### Installation Guide

1. Download the most up to date resource from keymaster.
2. Drag the "trase_gangs" into your resources. `(You can rename the resource)`
3. Open the "config.lua" and follow the configuration guide.
4. Add "ensure trase_gangs" to your server.cfg.
5. Make sure everything works as expected, for any support contact us via discord.
